## What was announced

Theo Hourmouzis is joining Anthropic as General Manager of Australia and New Zealand, marking the next step in our investment in the region. Hourmouzis will meet with customers and partners this week alongside executives from our global team, as we officially open our Sydney office.

Hourmouzis brings more than 20 years of leadership experience in the technology industry across Asia Pacific to the role. He joins us from Snowflake, where he most recently served as Senior Vice President for Australia, New Zealand and ASEAN, helping enterprise and public sector organisations across financial services, retail, aviation and government move AI from experimentation to business impact.
